About Me.

Hi! It's nice to meet you.

I am a graduate of UCSD and motivated by food, sci-fi, and superheroes. I was a high school English teacher working on a League of Legends themed app for fun, until I decided to try and turn that fun into a viable career. So I bet all my chips on this goal, quit my teaching job, and enrolled in LearningFuze, an intense 12-week full immersion accelerated web development bootcamp. Three months and at least 450 hours of coding later, I graduated from the bootcamp. The bet paid off.

I can sit in front of a computer for six hours straight and code as long as I have a cup of tea on my right, a bowl of watermelon on my left, and the Steven Universe theme song playing in the background. I think Picard and Janeway are tied for best Star Trek captain, and find it amusing that Legolas was in the Hobbit movies even though he was never even mentioned in the Hobbit book.

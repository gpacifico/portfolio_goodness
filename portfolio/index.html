About Me.

Hi! It's nice to meet you.

I am a graduate of UCSD and motivated by food, sci-fi, and superheroes. I was a high school English teacher building a League of Legends themed app for fun, until I decided to try and turn that fun into a viable career. But I knew I needed to level up my skills to make it happen. Books and online courses weren’t enough anymore, so I committed myself to this goal, quit my teaching job, enrolled and graduated from a full immersion accelerated web development bootcamp, and now happily spend my days programming.

I think it’s as close to magic as we can get when a blank text editor can be filled with lines of code and those lines turn into a game people can play to de-stress after a hard day, or a dating app singles can use to find love. I can sit in front of a computer for six hours straight and code as long as I have the Steven Universe theme song playing in the background. I think Picard and Janeway are tied for best Star Trek captain, and find it amusing that Legolas was in the Hobbit movies even though he was never even mentioned in the Hobbit book.
